About me:

I'm Nuara Costa, a teacher, CS enthusiast, lifelong learner passionate about human connection and learning. I hold an associate's in CS, a bachelor's degree in English Langugage & Literature, and currently about to start a Master's in Learning, Design & Technology (LDT) at Georgetown University. Creating learning experiences has been in my life since I started teaching, but I was not aware of it. Overtime, I started designing my own teaching materials and helping my students achieve their goals. In 2022, I decided to go to school in the US, which was then in Business Management, but soon Computer Science caught my eye and I switched fields. There, I realized how important good design is, and how learning happened in my brain. That's when I researched and found the LDT field. I became enamorated by finally putting vocabulary in something I had instinctively done, but finally knew how to express it. Need to improve your learning experience? Hit me up!
